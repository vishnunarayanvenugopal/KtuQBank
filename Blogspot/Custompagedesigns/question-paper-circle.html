About Post

This post contains the Question Papers for APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU - APJKTU) B.tech Students. The question papers for First Year, Second Year , Third Year and fourth year of the following branches are available.The computer science question papers is similar to iit computer science .
This page contains the Question papers for KTU students for the branches computer science engineering, Civil engineering , Electronics and communication,Electrical and electronics engineering, mechanical engineering and information Technology.
